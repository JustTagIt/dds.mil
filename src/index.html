Transforming technology within the
Department of Defense

The Defense Digital Service is an agency team of the U.S. Digital Service. Our mission is to improve government and support national defense through technology and design.

Solving Big Problems. Supporting Critical Missions.

The Department of Defense defends and protects our nation's military and civilians across the globe. However, oftentimes progress may be hindered by outdated systems and technology.

Our mission is to drive a giant leap forward in the way the agency builds, buys and deploys technology and digital services. We hire top technologists for short-term "tours of civic duty" to work alongside civil servants and service members building better tools to strengthen national security and care for military members and their families.

Our team

We hire world-class technologists, designers, product managers, and problem solvers from diverse background for short-term, high-impact ‘tours of duty’ at the Pentagon. Our team of nerds develop code, manage technical projects or product releases, rewire outdated IT approaches.

Join us

The best of technology. The best of government-- and we need you.

Civilian Positions: We need passionate designers, software engineers, product managers and others, who are committed to making the Department of Defense's critical systems simple, fast and easy to use. You'll be working alongside brilliant civil servants and tech folk from across government and industry.

The Defense Digital Service is an agency team of the U.S. Digital Service headquartered at the White House, so every application goes through the USDS hiring pipeline. When applying, please write that you are interested in joining the Defense Digital Service team in the "Anything Else" section.

We look forward to hearing from you!

Apply to USDS

DoD employees (both civilian and military personnel) with technical experience in software, hardware, and electrical engineering; hacking; software and product development are encouraged to apply for short-term details under DDS.
